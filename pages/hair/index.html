Indian gooseberry: Use Indian gooseberry / amla for fast natural hair growth. Amla consists of rich vitamin C. If you have deficiency in the body this may results to hair loss. So gooseberry can help to maintain a healthy scalp and gives good hair growth. Take gooseberry and make pulp. Mix this pulp with lemon juice and massage your scalp with this mixture. Keep this overnight and shampoo in the morning.

Daily Oiling
Massage the scalp daily at least for few minutes with lukewarm oil. You can use any type of oils for massaging like coconut oil, lavender oil, almond oil, mustard oil and jojoba oil. Due to dandruff also the hair may lose, in that condition jojoba oil gives best results. Sebum in the jojoba oil replaces the scalp. Massage with oil and shampoo the hair after 1 hour.

Henna for your hair
The traditional henna pack: Soak henna mixed with black tea overnight. In the morning, add two beaten eggs, four teaspoons of curd, juice of half a lemon and one tablespoon of coconut or olive oil. Leave this mask on for at least two hours before washing it off with cold water for best results. Use a mild shampoo.
